# B45 Labs – Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-01-01  
**Last updated:** 2026-06-27  
**Applies to:** B45 Labs Revit Add-in v2.0.0 (Revit 2023, 2024, 2025, 2026, 2027)

This Privacy Policy explains how B45 Labs (“B45 Labs”, “we”, “our”, “us”) collects and uses information when you use the B45 Labs Revit add-in (the “Software”).

## 1. Scope
This Policy applies to information collected through the Software — usage analytics and error logs used solely for internal product development, stability improvements, and user support.

## 2. What We Collect
The Software may collect the following categories of information. Collection is organized in **two tiers**:

- **Essential data** (sections 2.1–2.5) is collected by default to operate, support, and improve the Software. You can turn it off entirely with the **"Share anonymous usage data"** toggle in the in-app My Profile window.
- **Optional data** (section 2.6 — your user profile) is synced **only if you opt in** via the **"Also sync my user profile"** toggle in My Profile. It is **off by default**.

### 2.1 Usage Data
- Commands executed (name/identifier) and usage counts.
- Timestamped usage events (e.g., when a command was executed).
- Add-in version and build number, the Revit version (e.g., “Autodesk Revit 2026”), and the Revit interface language (e.g., English, Portuguese).

### 2.2 Error and Diagnostic Data
- Error messages and stack traces generated by the Software, and the command that was active when an error occurred.
- File paths, model file names, and email addresses are automatically redacted before this data leaves your machine.

### 2.3 Installation and Update Data
- First-seen and last-updated timestamps, the current add-in version, and the version an upgrade came from.

### 2.4 Pseudonymous Identifiers
- A randomly generated installation identifier (`install_id`) and session identifier (`session_id`). These contain no name, email, or personally identifiable information and are used only to group usage and error records for troubleshooting and analytics.

### 2.5 Region Data
- Country, continent, and region, derived from your IP address at the time of submission. This is coarse location only — never a street address or GPS coordinates — and your IP address itself is not stored. It is collected by default under the essential tier (legitimate interest); turning off **"Share anonymous usage data"** stops the IP lookup entirely. The lookup is performed by a third-party service (ipwhois.app).

### 2.6 User Profile Data (Voluntary — synced only if you opt in)
If you choose to fill out the optional in-app User Profile, we collect: full name, preferred name, email address, company/studio name, role/title, Revit experience, and how you found B45 Labs. Providing this information is entirely voluntary. Your profile is stored **encrypted** on your computer (`%AppData%\B45Labs\user-profile.enc`) and can be edited or erased at any time from the User Profile window. It is **synced to our servers only if you opt in** to the optional data tier (see section 8); otherwise it never leaves your computer.

## 3. What We Do NOT Collect
We do not intentionally collect any “sensitive personal data” (special categories), such as health data, biometric identifiers, precise location data, government IDs, financial account data, or authentication credentials.

We do not collect:
- Project file contents, drawings, geometry, or BIM data.
- Revit model or family file names, file paths, or folder locations.
- Payment information. Donations/support (if offered) are processed by third-party providers; B45 Labs does not receive or store payment details.
- Precise geolocation (street address / GPS coordinates).
- Passwords, authentication credentials, or personal communications.
- Your Revit or Windows username.
- Device or hardware specifications (processor, memory, screen resolution) or operating-system details.

If such information appears in a user-provided support screenshot or log, please redact it before sharing.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use collected information strictly for internal purposes, including:
- Measuring feature adoption and command usage to prioritize development and improvements.
- Improving stability, performance, and reliability across different Revit versions and environments.
- Debugging and fixing errors and regressions.
- Validating installer/update distribution health (e.g., adoption by version, compatibility signals).
- Producing aggregated, anonymized metrics (e.g., usage volume by command or region) for roadmap planning.

## 5. Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell your data and we do not share collected data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

We may use trusted service providers to store or process analytics (e.g., hosting or database services). Such providers are used only to support our internal operations and are not authorized to use the information for their own purposes.

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the security of the Software.

## 6. Data Retention
We retain data only as long as necessary for internal analytics, troubleshooting, and product improvement.

As a general guideline:
- Usage and diagnostics data may be retained for up to **24 months** unless a longer retention is required to investigate recurring issues or to comply with legal obligations.

## 7. Security
Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections, and the local user profile is encrypted at rest on your computer. Error data is sanitized to remove file paths, model file names, and email addresses before transmission. Collected data is stored on access-controlled infrastructure and is limited to authorized personnel for the purposes described in this Policy. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect collected information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 8. Your Choices and Requests
You control collection from the in-app **My Profile** window:
- **“Share anonymous usage data”** — master switch; turn it off to stop all telemetry collection (including coarse region).
- **“Also sync my user profile”** — optional tier, **off by default**; turn it on to sync the profile (name, email) you entered to our servers.
- **“Export my data”** — download a copy of everything stored locally about you (profile and usage history) as a JSON file.
- **“Erase my data”** — delete all locally stored B45 Labs data (profile, usage history, and machine identity) and request removal of your profile from our servers.

You may also contact us to:
- request information about data associated with your usage,
- request correction or deletion where applicable.

We may ask for additional information to verify your request.

## 9. International Use
The Software may be used in different countries. Information may be processed in the United States or other locations where our service providers operate.

## 10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the most recent revision. Material changes may be communicated through release notes or in-Software notices.
